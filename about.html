Tracing the threads of fate.

I do not believe in coincidence. The universe is a clockwork mechanism of infinite complexity, and every gear turns for a reason. My journey into Jyotishya began not out of curiosity, but out of a necessity to understand the "why" behind the chaos.

For over a decade, I have studied the ancient Vedic texts, translating their Sanskrit verses into the language of modern life. I function less as a fortune teller and more as a translator of cosmic code.

My role is to hand you the map you were born with, so you may navigate the labyrinth of your life with open eyes.

The Practice

How I Work

I

Chart as Diagnostic

The birth chart is not a crystal ball. It is a diagnostic tool — a snapshot of the sky at the moment of your first breath, encoding the karma you bring into this life.

II

Vedic Precision

I work with the Parashari system of Jyotish, using the sidereal zodiac — the actual star positions at birth, not the tropical approximation of Western astrology.

III

Context, Not Fate

A reading provides context. It does not seal your fate. Understanding your patterns gives you the power to work with them consciously rather than being swept along unconsciously.

"
Destiny is not a cage. It is a river. You cannot stop the water from flowing, but you can learn to swim with the current rather than drowning against it.
